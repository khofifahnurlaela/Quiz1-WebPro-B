KEBUMEN BERIMAN

Bersih Indah Manfaat Aman dan Nyaman

Kebumen is a regency in the southern part of the Indonesian province Central Java. This regency is bordered by Banjanegara in the North, Wonosobo and Purworejo in the East, the Indian Ocean in the South, and Cilacap and Banyumas in the West.

The southern part of Kebumen is lowland, while in the northern part of the mountains and hills which are part of the series of the South Serayu Mountains. Kebumen has a coastline of 53 KM which is mostly a beach with a sand dune.

There is an area in this regency which is used for geology research, namely Karangsambung.
