the place you must visit

BENTENG VAN DER WICJK

Fort Van Der Wijck is a Dutch East Indies fortress that was built in the 18th century. This fort is located in the city of Gombong. Over time, this fortress has become one of the historical tourist destinations frequented by tourists and local residents.

MENGANTI BEACH

Menganti Beach is located in Karangduwur Village, Ayah, Kebumen, Central Java. This beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Central Java. The sand beaches are white and there is a panoramic view of the hills as well as beautiful karst cliffs. The hilly landscape makes it a little difficult to access this beach. Before reaching the beach via a winding road, however the view is still amazing. The karst mountains are beautiful, hilly cones, and the sea is occasionally seen while on the road that creeps up the hill.

TUGU LAWET

Tugu Lawet is a symbolic sign that the people of Kebumen at one time experienced a triumph in managing the lawet's nest so that it became a source of regional income.

PENTULU INDAH

On this hill, you can see the sunrise against the background of the mist of Mount Sindoro and Mount Sumbing. To get to this location, you don't need to worry about doing heavy hiking, because this hill only has an altitude of 300 meters above sea level, so this tour is highly recommended for your family.
